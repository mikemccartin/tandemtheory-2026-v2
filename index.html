The Better Client Experience

When we set out to build the agency, it was important to me that we did it from a client's point of view. I felt that too many agencies had become internally focused and had forgotten what it was actually like to be a client. Being a client is hard work, and the last thing they need is an agency that makes it even harder.

I wanted to create an agency that respected that reality. We offered simple, easy-out contracts because, after all, there is no point in forcing a client to work with us if they don't want to. I wanted our team to understand that the real job was delivering a steady stream of smart, business-building ideas—ideas that actually moved the needle.

I also believed strongly in putting the right technology in place so clients could easily see, understand, and track the progress of their work. Transparency mattered. Just as important, I wanted to hire people who believed, as I do, that clients want to know how much you care before they care how much you know. People who valued trust, empathy, and the ability to build close, personal relationships with their clients.

That philosophy became a guiding principle inside the agency. We call it BCE—Better Client Experience. We talk about it every day. We measure it constantly through ongoing client feedback. And we hold ourselves accountable to it, knowing that we can always do better and always find new ways to serve our clients more effectively.

Results

We set out to build an agency with an unprecedented level of accountability to our clients.

From the beginning, our belief was simple: every client dollar should be treated as if it were our own. That meant carefully and consistently measuring the true bottom-line impact of everything we do. Too often, we had seen agencies hide behind soft, fuzzy metrics that failed to track performance all the way through the conversion funnel. We knew there had to be a better way.

So we built a best-in-class marketing analytics group—staffed with exceptional data scientists and analysts capable of stitching together disparate data sources to measure true conversion and attribution. Transparency and accountability weren't add-ons to our process; they became foundational to how we work and a cornerstone of our Better Client Experience promise.

Today, our team is fluent in all leading reporting and visualization platforms, including Tableau, Domo, Google Looker Studio, and Microsoft Power BI. More importantly, we believe there is no stronger way to demonstrate our value than by providing clients with a continuous stream of insight into what's working, what's not, and—critically—why.

Culture

I wanted to build a culture-first agency—one that values human decency and real work-life balance. I believed then, and still believe now, that if you get the culture right, everything else follows. Great culture attracts great talent, and great talent produces work that delivers meaningful results for clients. That belief came directly from my own experiences at other agencies, and it's what pushed me to do things differently.

At Tandem, we've wrapped our culture around three core values: teamwork, transparency, and trust. These aren't just words on a wall—they're the foundation of how we operate every day. We look for "we" people, not "I" people. We believe in giving and receiving honest, direct feedback. And we trust that when you hire the right people, you don't need to micromanage their time or watch the clock. The right people do the right things, the right way.

I'm proud that our people don't carry that familiar pit in their stomach on Sunday night, dreading the week ahead. I lived that life for years, and I promised myself I'd never recreate it. Work should be challenging and meaningful—but it shouldn't come at the expense of your health, your family, or your sense of self.

We're on the right track, and we're continuing to grow with intention. And, I believe our people, and the culture they create, are a key part of how we deliver a Better Client Experience. We're always looking for talented, like-minded people who believe, like I did, that this business doesn't have to be a painful grind.

Technology

I've always believed that technology, when applied thoughtfully, can help us deliver a Better Client Experience. From the start, I wanted to build systems that would give our clients real-time visibility into their work and enable our teams to move faster and smarter.

Over the years, we've developed a portfolio of proprietary tools that set us apart. These aren't off-the-shelf solutions—they're purpose-built platforms designed to solve real problems we saw in the industry. Tools that automate the tedious, surface the important, and free our people to focus on strategy and creativity.

Today, we're leaning heavily into AI—not as a buzzword, but as a genuine force multiplier. We're integrating machine learning into our analytics, our creative workflows, and our campaign optimization. The agencies that figure out how to blend human creativity with AI capability will define the next decade, and we intend to be at the forefront.
